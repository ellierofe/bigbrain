## Decisions made
- **`ContentPane` is the named working area pattern** ‚Äî every page: `PageHeader` + sub-chrome (tabs, switchers, badges) outside, `ContentPane` wraps the scrollable working area. Documented in component JSDoc and registry. All future pages must follow this.
- **"DNA" not "Strategy"** throughout nav and UI. "Strategy" label retired.
- **Sidebar expand on icon click** ‚Äî clicking a section icon while collapsed expands the sidebar and opens that section (rather than requiring Cmd+B first).
- **Hover-to-expand CSS attempt deferred** ‚Äî CSS-only `:has()` approach had partial effect but didn't feel reliable. Revisit with a proper `onMouseEnter`/`onMouseLeave` approach in a future session.
- **IBM Plex Sans** replaces Geist as the app font.
- **Root `app/page.tsx` deleted** ‚Äî `(dashboard)/page.tsx` now owns `/` cleanly. No redirect layer needed.
