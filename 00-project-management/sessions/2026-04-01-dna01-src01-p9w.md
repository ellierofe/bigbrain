## What was done

**Migrate bug fix:**
- Root cause identified: migrations 0004 and 0005 were applied manually to Neon but never recorded in `drizzle.__drizzle_migrations`. Drizzle saw them as pending, diffed the schema, found the tables already existed, and silently exited with no error.
- Fix: computed SHA256 hashes of both migration files (`node -e` + `crypto.createHash`) and inserted them directly into `drizzle.__drizzle_migrations` with the `created_at` timestamps from `_journal.json`. All 6 migrations now tracked.
- `drizzle-kit generate` continues to work correctly. The `db:migrate` step still has the SSL/pg driver issue — the MCP apply pattern remains in use.
